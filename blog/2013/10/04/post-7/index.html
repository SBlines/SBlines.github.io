End of Week 3

I feel like we’re really getting into things now and am trying to think ahead so that I’m not wondering ‘where did the time go?’ once we get to December. I miss this place already.

Stackworld was a lot of fun this week. My favorite part was the interaction, that there was more to it than typing a few things and just looking at the output. I was able to customize the player pages a bit and felt really good helping others do the same. For once I wasn’t poking around trying to see who could help me, instead a few guys were gathered around my screen to look at the code. I spent some freetime creating rooms and have more of the world planned out.

I barely got any prep work done this week. I’ve been too tired by the time I leave that I can’t concentrate on anything else. Even reading the web-scraping info last night was taking longer than it should have. I’ll take another look at Ruby Bits where I was stuck, and possibly the Treehouse Ruby tomorrow.

Maybe at some point I’ll look into a comments section for the blog.
