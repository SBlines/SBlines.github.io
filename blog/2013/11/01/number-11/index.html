End of Week 7

I don’t remember what we did the beginning of this week. jQuery which I liked doing and probaby Trip Planner. Once Rails was set up I was able to get to work and cash in $$$$. The way I set up the different days wasn’t the DRYest but it worked and I was able to get it done within reasonable time. For the first time using jQuery, I just wanted things to work.

Ajax will be dealt with, still trying to figure out the Code School course but I’ll work on it this weekend. I was only watching some of the videos, without sound, which isn’t the best way to learn.

My Code School rank is up there and after this weekend I’ll be on top and finally take Julian’s spot. I shouldn’t let this distract me from Treehouse, but it does.

We’ve been doing Exercism problems in the morning which is a nice way to star the morning. They’re not too intense which helps ease into the day. Besides the actual test parameters, only today did I have some trouble with Nucleotide Count. The hash wasn’t updating as expected.

I’ve been feeling like less of an impostor after Game of Life and Trip Planner, aka less Ruby/Rails which only means I need to keep working on them. I continued to work on Michael Hartl’s tutorial last weekend but felt like I didn’t accomplish much despite new lines of code. This weekend I’m going to work on Ajax, Backbone, and JavaScript.

I’d like to focus on frontend for the project. I have a few ideas and it comes down to being able to push myself just enough to stay within the time limit. I will move on from Gosu to stick with JS.

Missed yoga today, by the time I saw the shoes outside the session was 10 minutes in. I could have jumped in but I’ll do some stretching later. I came in late today which is maybe why I didn’t know we’d be doing yoga. Instead, I went outside to watch a movie in the making. I really wanted to tell Keanu Reeves that I know Kung Fu. I was casted for two scenes in The Dark Knight Rises in 2011. I took the whole week off from work for costuming and rehearsals except the scenes ended up getting canceled before anything started. Then I was asked to do another shot but ended up not going as it was only a few days’ notice and I had plans to go to a concert. When I saw Electric Six play the next time, I was able to tell the singer how I skipped being in the movie for their show.

I’m really hungry so I’m going to stop typing now.
