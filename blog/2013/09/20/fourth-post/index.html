I enjoyed my week here at Fullstack and am looking forward to the rest of my time here. Some of the Ruby has been difficult. While I didn’t do most of the testing, it didn’t look so difficult when we went over the problems and I was able to see how the programs ran. I think that with time I will become more aware of what Ruby can do and how to make it happen. I have since begun the Haiku project, which doesn’t seem as difficult as I had initially thought. I liked being a part of a team and it was interesting to see how Vin and Scott think in relation to the code. I found that Vin and I had a more similar approach. I am interested in working with the rest of the students but feel I should be more prepared next time, in order to contribute more to the code.
