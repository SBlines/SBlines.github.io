While waiting for 1-on-1 chats to continue I focused on Codecademy’s Ruby work. If I can get more of the general concepts under control, I’ll have an easier time with anything Ruby-related rather than the specific tasks needed for the exercises. While the exercises are a good way to prove what I know by putting my knowledge to use, there’s not so much knowledge available right now. I’m working on the Object-Oriented Programming I section with classes and being able to read about them while practicing the lesson in repl seems to be working. As frustrating as things can get I kept in mind that I only need time to work at my own pace (while hoping that I don’t become severely out-paced by the curriculum). Regardless of how my learning progress compares to the rest of the class, I’ll get things done. This downtime has already proved to be very worthwhile and there is still an hour before I go in for the chat.
